all ints are big-endian

voter -> CLA:
can send 1 message:
    
    REQ_VALIDATION_NUM = 0x1


CLA -> voter:
can send 2 messages:
    
    VALIDATION_NUM || validation num
        (byte=0x1)      (8 byte int)
    
    UNREGISTERED_VOTER
        (byte=0x2)


voter -> CTF:
can send 2 messages:
    
    VOTE           || random voter id || validation num || ballot_size || filled ballot
        (byte=0x1)       (8 bytes)         (8 bytes)        (4 bytes)     (ballot_size bytes)
    
    BALLOT_REQUEST
        (byte=0x2)


CTF -> voter:
can send 4 messages:
    
    VOTE_SUCCESS
        (byte=0x1)
    
    BALLOT_RESPONSE || ballot_size || ballot
        (byte=0x2)      (4 bytes)       (ballot_size bytes)
        ballot can be bytes describing a json-formatted dict,
        or whatever the CTF wants to use

    INVALID_VALIDATION_NUM
        (byte=0x3)

    VALIDATION_NUM_ALREADY_USED
        (byte=0x4)
